Visit Evergreen

Enjoy nature, culture, and the outdoors in Colorado's foothills.

Top three activities in Evergreen

Sighting local wildlife

Elk, deer, foxes, and bear are common sights around Evergreen.

Parks

Evergreen has several parks and local peaks to hike. Bergen Peak's elevation is 9,708 feet.

Shopping and Dining

Downtown Evergreen has a variety of local shops and restaurants to enjoy along Bear Creek Road.
